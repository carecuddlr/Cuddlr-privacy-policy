# Privacy Policy for Fit in a Minute

**Last Updated: January 22, 2025**

## Your Privacy Matters

Fit in a Minute ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our fitness application.

We believe in transparency and minimal data collection. All your fitness data stays on your device â€” we do not have servers that store your personal information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
Optional profile information (name and gender) provided during onboarding to personalize your experience. This data is stored only on your device.

### Fitness Activity Data
Exercise completions, workout durations, workout history (last 90 days), recently completed exercises (last 15), streaks, achievements, and milestones. All stored locally on your device. Note: All calorie burn estimates and activity metrics are approximations only and should not be considered medically accurate measurements.

### App Preferences
Notification settings, reminder times, workout preferences, and app usage patterns.

### Challenge Progress
Daily and weekly challenge completions and progress tracking to help you stay motivated.

### Jumbo Workouts
Unlock history, ad watch counts, and access duration for premium Jumbo workout features.

---

## 2. How We Store Your Data

### Local Storage Only
All your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do NOT have servers that collect or store your data.

### No Cloud Sync
Your fitness data is not uploaded to any cloud service. If you uninstall the app or change devices, your data will not transfer.

### Data Retention
Workout history is retained for the last 90 days to provide accurate streak and progress tracking. Data is automatically cleaned up beyond this period.

---

## 3. Advertising

### Google AdMob
We display advertisements through Google AdMob to support the app's development. This includes interstitial, rewarded, banner, and native ads.

### Ad Data Collection
Google may collect information about your device and ad interactions as described in Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Ad Frequency Limits
We implement reasonable limits on ad frequency (currently capped at 20 ads per day with 5-minute intervals) to ensure a positive user experience.

### Rewarded Ads
You may optionally watch rewarded ads to unlock premium workout features. This is entirely voluntary.

---

## 4. Analytics & Crash Reporting

### Firebase Analytics
We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data to understand how users engage with the app. This includes exercise completions, workout sessions, streak achievements, challenge progress, jumbo workout usage, and feature interactions. All data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to individual users.

### Firebase Crashlytics
We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information when the app encounters technical issues. This includes device information, app version, and stack traces. This helps us identify and fix bugs quickly to improve app stability and user experience.

### What We Track
We track app usage patterns including: exercise completions, workout durations, calories burned estimates, streak milestones, challenge completions, feature usage (Jumbo workouts, Quick picks), notification interactions, and app performance metrics. We do NOT track your location, contacts, phone number, email, or any personally identifiable information.

### Data Usage
Analytics data helps us understand which features are most popular, identify technical issues, improve workout recommendations, and make the app better for everyone. Crash data helps us maintain a stable, bug-free experience.

### Opt-Out
Analytics and crash reporting are enabled by default to help us improve the app. All data is anonymized and aggregated. You may be able to limit certain ad personalization through your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking, Android: Settings > Google > Ads).

## 5. Permissions We Request

### Notifications
Used to send daily workout reminders at your preferred time. You can disable this in app settings.

### Vibration
Used for haptic feedback during workouts and notifications to enhance your experience.

### Boot Completion

Your personal information and fitness data are never sold, rented, or traded to third parties.

## 7. Your Rights & Data Control

### Data Ownership
All your fitness data belongs to you and is stored only on your device.

### Data Deletion
You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling removes everything.

### No Account Required
We do not require you to create an account or provide an email address to use the app.

### Internet Connectivity
The app requires an internet connection to function properly, particularly for displaying advertisements. Your fitness data is never transmitted over the internet and remains stored locally on your device.
